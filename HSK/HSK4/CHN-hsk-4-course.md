### New grammar rules

#### 肯定 kěndìng (certainly; definitely)

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|--|--|--|
| 等活动结束的时候人肯定更多。 | Děng huódòng jiéshù de shíhou rén kěndìng gèng duō. | There will definitely be more people when the event ends. |
| 上下班的时间路上肯定堵车。 | Shàng xiàbān de shíjiān lùshang kěndìng dǔchē. | There will definitely be traffic jams during rush hour. |

#### 提前 tíqián (in advance; ahead of time)

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|--|--|--|
| 你还是提前一点出发吧。 | Nǐ háishi tíqián yīdiǎn chūfā ba. | You should set off a bit earlier. |
| 如果有变化，请提前告诉我。 | Rúguǒ yǒu biànhuà, qǐng tíqián gàosu wǒ. | If there are any changes, please let me know in advance. |

#### 按照 ànzhào (according to)

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|--|--|--|
| 按照广播的提醒到车门旁边等着下车。 | Ànzhào guǎngbō de tíxǐng dào chēmén pángbiān děngzhe xià chē. | According to the announcement, stand by the door to wait for your stop. |
| 你为什么没按照经理的要求做？ | Nǐ wèishéme méi ànzhào jīnglǐ de yāoqiú zuò? | Why didn't you follow the manager's instructions? |
