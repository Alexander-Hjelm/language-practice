## Lesson notes

### Newbie: All the Way to Intermediate 1

#### #1 How to Say Hello (2025-01-08)

早！
zǎo!
Morning!

Particles:

早 (zǎo): early

---

好久不见！
hǎojiǔ bùjiàn!
Long time no see!

Particles:

好 (hǎo): good, very
久 (jiǔ): long (in terms of time)
不 (bù): not, no
见 (jiàn): to see, to meet

#### #2 How to Say Goodbye in Chinese (2025-01-08)

拜拜！
bàibài！
Bye!

Particles:

拜 (bài): "to bow" or "to pay respects." Here used to mimic the sound of the english "Bye".

---

先走了。
xiān zǒu le。
I'll head off first.

Particles:

先 (xiān): "first" or "ahead
走 (zǒu): "to walk" or "to leave"
了 (le): A particle indicating a change of state or completed action

---

慢走。
màn zǒu。
Take care (lit: walk slowly)

Particles:

慢 (màn): "slow" or "slowly
走 (zǒu): "to walk" or "to leave"

#### #3 Asking Questions Politely (2025-01-08)

请问这个多少钱？
qǐngwèn zhè ge duōshǎo qián？
May I ask how much this is?

---

请问你会说英文吗？
qǐngwèn nǐ huì shuō Yīngwén ma？
May I ask if you speak English?

---

请给我这个。
qǐng gěi wǒ zhè ge。
Please can you give me this.

---

Particles:

请 (qǐng): Please, to invite, to request
问 (wèn): To ask
会 (huì): Can, to know how to, will
英 (yīng): England, hero, outstanding
文 (wén): Language, culture, writing

#### #4 Apologizing and Responding to Apologies (2025-01-08)

---

---

没事

Don't worry about it.

---

不会。
bùhuì。
It's okay; don't mention it (Tw)

---

Particles:

没 (méi): not, no
关 (guān): to close, to shut, or related to
系 (xì): connection, department, system
意 (yì): meaning, intention, idea
思 (sī): to think, thought
事 (shì): thing, matter, affair

#### #5 Thank You & You're Welcome (2025-01-08)

不客气。
bù kèqì。
you're welcome.

---

应该的。
yīnggāi de。
I ought to have done that (lit); you're most welcome; it was a pleasure.

Particles:

客 (kè): guest, visitor, customer
气 (qì): air, gas, spirit, energy
应 (yīng): should, ought to, respond
该 (gāi): should, ought to, this, that
